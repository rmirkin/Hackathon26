How does this work?

This detector was trained on real AI-generated voice samples and real human recordings. It extracts 23 audio features and compares them to learned AI and human profiles using importance-weighted distance.

Top signals: Silence patterns, pitch mean/variation, volume dynamics, spectral flatness, and harmonic structure are the strongest differentiators.

The model runs entirely client-side using the Web Audio API. No audio data is sent anywhere.
