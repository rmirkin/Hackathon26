Detection Indicators

How does this work?

This detector analyzes audio features that differ between AI-generated and real human speech — entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API.

Pitch Variation: Real humans vary their pitch dynamically (std ~70Hz). AI voices tend to be flatter (~38Hz).

Volume Dynamics: Human speech has large volume swings (rms_std ~0.10). AI is more uniform (~0.05).

Spectral Flatness: AI-generated audio often has a flatter, noisier spectrum. Human speech is more tonal.

Silence Patterns: AI TTS often inserts mechanical pauses (~35% silence). Humans have less dead air (~14%).

Harmonic Structure: AI tends to have stronger harmonics relative to percussive elements.
