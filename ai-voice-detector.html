Detection Indicators

How does this work?

This detector analyzes audio features that differ between AI-generated and real human speech — entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API.

Jitter & Shimmer: Real voices have natural micro-perturbations in pitch and volume. AI voices are unnaturally smooth.

Spectral patterns: AI-generated audio tends to have a "too clean" frequency profile, lacking the subtle noise and harmonics of real speech.

Pitch dynamics: Humans vary pitch naturally with emotion and emphasis. AI tends toward more predictable intonation patterns.

Silence & breathing: Humans breathe, pause, and hesitate. Many AI voices produce continuous speech without natural gaps.
