How does this work?

This page renders hidden text in various font families and measures the resulting pixel dimensions. If a font is installed on your system, the text renders differently than the fallback font — revealing which fonts you have.

The combination of installed fonts varies between operating systems, language packs, and software you've installed (like Adobe Creative Suite, Microsoft Office, etc). This makes your font set a surprisingly effective fingerprint.

Combined with other signals (canvas, WebGL, screen size), font fingerprinting can identify users with over 99% accuracy — no cookies required.
